Understanding bible-codex

Two hard things at once — the biblical concepts and the architecture built on them — taught together, in short modules, with diagrams and games.

What this app is (in one breath)

A local-first, literary-beautiful Bible that turns reading into discovery. Scripture is typeset to its literary form (narrative as prose, poetry as poetry), read as a horizontal scroll or vertically. You mark it like a physical Bible — and your marks survive every change of font, layout, and translation. Tablet + desktop, offline. (Full philosophy → VISION.md.)

How the course is structured

Five modules, each a short session of its own so nothing runs too long. Each module pairs the biblical idea with the app mechanic built on it, and ends with an interactive game.
